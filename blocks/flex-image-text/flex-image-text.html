Last Keystroke Prioritization (LKP) & Snap Click

LKP allows seamless directional changes by prioritizing the last key pressed, ensuring smoother gameplay in titles like Valorant. With Snap Click, deeper keypresses take precedence when two keys are hit at once, automatically resetting shallower presses. Together, these features make counter-strafing effortless, giving you the accuracy and speed needed for high-level FPS play. *They are disabled by default, but you can easily enable them in our Launcher web app when needed.
